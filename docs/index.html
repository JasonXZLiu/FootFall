Our Mission

“Generating green energy through footprints and reducing Canadian energy footprints one step at a time.”

FootFall Inc.

What We Do

FootFall is dedicated to providing its customers with a simple yet effective way to go green! Our products and services are designed to meet the needs of businesses across industries, making the world more sustainable one step at a time.

Energy Production

Our research and design team has developed sophisticated systems that harness every bit of energy possible in each footstep.

Customizability

We offer multiple tile surfaces that easily integrate into existing interior design. No more endless searches for the ideal flooring - our product frequently bring new flooring options to market.

Payback

Your investment in FootFall practically pays itself off - our advanced technology is capable of producing energy that can significantly reduce energy bills and save money.

Installation

The FootFall Tile has been designed in a modular fashion, allowing for quick and easy installation. However large or small your business is, FootFall is your go to for green energy solutions.

Insights

Our technology has been designed to provide you with optimal functionality - through the implementation of sensors in the FootFall tile, businesses have the ability to gain valuable insights into foot traffic, activity, and energy distribution.

Satisfaction

With society currently shifting towards a more eco-friendly stance, FootFall provides businesses with a reliable way of going green. With a quick purchase and installation, your business can help meet green policies and standards for years to come.

Our Purpose

This business was established because for the John Dobson Entrepreneurship Cup. SHAD has an annual competition to create a product or service to solve or based on a specific theme. This year, the theme was, "How might Canadians reduce their individual energy footprint." Thus, FootFall was started and here we are today.
